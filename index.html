Expected Results
Socio Economic Partners

Civilization is a stage in the development of society. Before revealing the theme of the Project, we focus on our vision of the social principles of international unity at the current stage of development of society, as they will be the unifying factors in the process of implementing the Project.

We want to change this world for the better: to preserve and accelerate the economic, physical and moral evolutionary process of uman development and therefore we start ith ourselves in the hope that our initiative will acquire constructive meaning, and the goals of the Project will be accepted by the world community.

  1. Worldwide recognition of the factors influencing the viability of our planet as the property of all mankind. They are subject to consolidated accounting, are possible to maintain and improve their quality and are the main criteria for expressing the value of international settlement units (cash). And this is a direct path to the transition to a resource-oriented world economy.
  2. Restoring the environment by repurposing human values and ensuring adequate funding for restoration processes.
  3. Reconstruction of cities, transportation systems, agricultural and industrial enterprises into energy-efficient, environmentally friendly systems that can meet the needs of all people.
  4. Interchange of technologies and their use for the benefit of all peoples by streamlining the turnover of scientific and technical potential.
  5. Encouragement of creativity and creativity in a person in all its manifestations through the proposed methodology.
  6. Getting rid of remnants and prejudices by increasing the level of education of the Earth's population by creating a correlation model (without replacement) of linear and non-linear education, as well as rehabilitation and restoration of the cultural heritage of our civilization.
  7. Development of methodologies through scientific research, not random opinions, by creating a favorable environment and modern equipment with high-tech solutions for such developments.
  8. Providing people not only with everything necessary for life, but also educating individuality with the help of tasks that stimulate the human mind.
  9. Intellectual and emotional preparation of people for the upcoming changes.
  1. Four exchange indices, critical for modern society (INDEX Water Resource®, INDEX Land Fertility®, INDEX Forest Land®, INDEX Ocean Aptitude®), characterizing the viability of our civilization, will be created and put into international trading.
  2. A financial instrument (INDEX Viability®) will be created and put into international trading, having immunity to any speculative influences, which will become the Single Equivalent of the Value of any assets and bring their value to fair values.
  3. A self-regulatory organization will be created (the INDEX Viability® Regulatory Commission), which has the potential to become a prototype of the International Organization for the Regulation of the Circulation of Digital Means of Mutual Settlement.
  4. Blockchain technology, which is relevant for the world community, will be finalized and implemented, containing a fundamentally new mechanism for distributing commission fees for participating in the confirmation of a transaction and the Protocol on their AML compliance with the preservation of decentralized functions of the technology itself.
  5. Emission, distribution (between the actual owners) and the circulation of crypto currency (Viability Assurance Currency®) will be carried out, capable of becoming an asset of the State Reserves.
  6. The Reserve Fund will be created and filled, which will ensure that the Factors influencing the Viability of our civilization do not worsen and increase, as well as provide sufficient capital to finance Projects focused on the social and technological growth of society.
  7. An international open e-commerce portal (UNIFIN E-COMMERCE PORTAL®) will be put into operation, which will ensure the organization of electronic trading in international assets of the stock and commodity markets. Including on trading platforms and financial instruments of UNIFIN GROUP® companies within the framework of this Project.
  8. The methodology for calculating the current actual value of the money of the countries of the world using the Single Value Equivalent (INDEX Viability®) and with the possibility of replacing them with a Single Digital Payment Tool will be submitted for discussion to the world community.

Socio-Economic Viability Project

SOL LUCET OMNIBUS

The desire of people to enrich themselves is natural, since the sufficiency of funds determines the possibility of achieving the desired comfort in life. The implementation of the Project will ensure a change in priorities. Now it will be profitable to invest knowledge, experience, efforts and money in what is necessary for the full development of our civilization.

The chaos of the modern world economy has led to a lack of understanding of actual costs. The implementation of the Project ensures that this situation is put in order and creates a Single Value Equivalent for everyday circulation, which can neither be falsified nor used by anyone for personal purposes. It creates an opportunity to understand the actual value of any asset and preserve the purchasing power of the population's earned money.

The lack of security of banknotes of the countries of the world, the popularization of the circulation of unsecured digital means of mutual settlements, as well as the lack of control over their issue, led to a global crisis. The implementation of the Project will ensure the introduction of the Ideal means of payment, which will reformat the world monetary system and put it in order.

The lack of sustainable enough funding for the social and scientific spheres hinders the worthy development of our civilization. The implementation of the policy of formation and circulation of the Reserve Fund in the Draft will correct this situation once and for all.

The lack of a clear understanding among people of the effective and profitable application of experience and knowledge does not allow us to form a sustainable dynamics of the socio-technological development of our society. The implementation of the Project provides the rules of action and creates a huge number of directions for the realization of human potential.

The implementation of the Project allows the world community, regardless of the level of financial well-being of countries, to resist poverty, hunger, lack of necessary for life and other social and environmental problems.
